Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the delivery time?

Usually, shipping takes 3–5 business days within the country. During high-demand periods (like promotional campaigns), it might take a bit longer.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept most common payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), PayPal, and direct bank transfers.

Can I return an item I'm not satisfied with?

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day return policy from the day you receive your order. Please see our Return Policy for detailed information on how to proceed.

Do you offer trackable shipping?

Yes, all our shipments come with a tracking number. You will receive an email with tracking information once your order leaves our warehouse.

Do you have any discounts or coupon codes?

From time to time, we run special promotions and coupon codes. Keep an eye on our website or follow us on social media to stay updated.

How do I change my shipping address?

If you need to update your address before the order has been shipped, please contact us as soon as possible. If it has already been dispatched, you may need to reach out to the shipping provider directly.
